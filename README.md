# Customer Segmentation & Sales Analysis

## Overview
This project focuses on performing **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on customer sales data to uncover purchasing behavior, identify high-value customer segments, and generate business recommendations to improve revenue and customer experience.

---

## Objectives
- Improve customer experience by analyzing sales patterns
- Increase revenue using data-driven insights

---

## Tools & Libraries Used
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## Key Analysis Performed

### 1. Gender Analysis
- Female customers generated significantly higher revenue compared to male customers.
- Female contribution: **₹74.3M**
- Male contribution: **₹31.9M**

#### Insight
Female customers are the primary revenue-driving segment.

---

### 2. Age Group Analysis
Top contributing age groups:
- **26–35 years** → Highest revenue contributor (~₹42.6M)
- Followed by:
  - 36–45 years
  - 18–25 years

#### Insight
Young and mid-age working professionals form the core customer base.

---

### 3. State-Wise Sales Analysis
Top states by order volume:
- Uttar Pradesh
- Maharashtra
- Karnataka

#### Insight
High order volume does not always mean high revenue. Customer spending behavior differs by region.

---

### 4. Marital Status Analysis
- Married women contributed the highest share of purchases.

#### Insight
This customer segment shows strong and consistent purchasing behavior.

---

### 5. Occupation Analysis
Top contributing professions:
- IT
- Healthcare
- Aviation

#### Insight
Customers working in stable, higher-income professions contribute more revenue.

---

### 6. Product Category Analysis
Top-performing product categories:
- Food
- Clothing
- Electronics

#### Insight
Customers spend heavily on essential and lifestyle products.

---

## Business Recommendations

### Target High-Value Customers
Focus marketing efforts on:
- Females aged 26–35
- Working professionals
- Customers from high-performing states

### Increase Average Order Value (AOV)
- Product bundling
- Cross-selling electronics and accessories

### Regional Inventory Optimization
- Increase inventory in high-demand states
- Improve delivery efficiency

### Segment-Based Pricing
- Introduce premium products in higher-spending regions

### Customer Retention Strategies
- Loyalty programs
- Personalized marketing campaigns

---

## Conclusion
The analysis shows that the most valuable customer segment consists of:
- Married women
- Aged 26–35
- Working in professional sectors
- Located in high-demand states

Aligning marketing, pricing, and inventory strategies around this segment can significantly improve customer experience and drive long-term revenue growth.
